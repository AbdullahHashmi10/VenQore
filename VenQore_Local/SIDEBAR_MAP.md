# Project Sidebar Navigation Map

This document outlines the complete structure of the application's sidebar navigation, including Main Categories, Sub-Menus (Siblings), and a description of each page's functionality.

---

## 1. 🏠 Home
*Central hub for application overview and quick actions.*

*   **Quick Access**: Shortcuts to frequently used features.
*   **Dashboard**: Visual overview of business metrics.

---

## 2. 🛒 Sell (Outbound)
*Management of all customer-facing sales transactions.*

### Transactions
*   **Orders**: Comprehensive history of all finalized Sales Invoices.
*   **Quotations / Pre-Sales**: Non-binding price estimates.
*   **Proposals**: Formal business proposals.

### Post-Sale
*   **Returns History**: Log of Sales Returns (Credit Notes).
*   **Invoice Reminders**: Track unpaid invoices and reminders.
*   **Recurring Invoices**: Manage automatic subscription invoices.

### Config
*   **E-Invoicing**: Electronic invoice generation settings.

---

## 3. 🛍️ Purchase (Inbound)
*Management of vendor-facing transactions.*

### Transactions
*   **Purchases**: History of confirmed Purchase Invoices/Bills.
*   **Pre-Purchases**: (Purchase Orders) Request stock from suppliers.

### Post-Purchase
*   **Debit Notes**: Purchase Returns and financial adjustments.

---

## 4. 📦 Stock (Inventory)
*Inventory Management and Manufacturing.*

### Catalog
*   **Products**: Master database of items.
*   **Categories**: Product hierarchy.
*   **Attributes**: Product variants (Size, Color).
*   **Labels**: Barcode printing tool.

### Operations
*   **Stock Levels**: Quantity-on-hand view.
*   **Stock Operations**: Adjustments and write-offs.
*   **Stock Transfers**: Movement between warehouses.
*   **Stock Audit**: Physical inventory counting.

### Tracking
*   **Batch Tracking**: Expiry and batch management.
*   **Serial Tracking**: Individual item lifecycle tracking.

### Manufacturing
*   **Production**: Convert raw materials to finished goods.
*   **Cookbook**: Recipes/BOMs.

---

## 5. 👥 Contacts
*Relationship management.*

### Partners
*   **Customers**: Directory of buyers.
*   **Suppliers**: Directory of vendors.
*   **Parties**: Unified contact list.

### Team
*   **Staff Attendance**: Employee time tracking.
*   **Staff Summaries**: Performance reports.

---

## 6. 💰 Money (Finance)
*Financial tracking and analysis.*

### Cash Flow
*   **Payments**: Money Out.
*   **Expenses**: Operational costs.
*   **Receivables**: Money In (Debtors).
*   **Payables**: Money Owed (Creditors).

### Banking
*   **Fund Management**: Cash flow control.
*   **Bank Accounts**: Internal accounts/wallets.
*   **Bank Reconciliation**: Statement matching.

### Accounting
*   **Chart of Accounts**: Financial account structure.

---

## 7. ✨ Marketing
*   **Campaigns**: SMS/Email marketing.
*   **Online Store**: E-commerce settings.
*   **WooCommerce Sync**: External integration.

---

## 8. 📈 Insights (Reports)
*Comprehensive analytics.*

### Financial Health
*   **Profit & Loss**, **Balance Sheet**, **Cash Flow**, **Tax Report**.

### Sales Analysis
*   **Sales Report**, **Discount Report**, **Sale Aging**.

### Purchase Analysis
*   **Purchase Report**, **Expense Report**.

### Inventory Intelligence
*   **Stock Valuation**, **Low Stock**, **Movement History**, **Expiry Report**.

### Operational
*   **Activity Log**: User action audit trail (Also in System).

---

## 9. ⚙️ System
*   **Settings**: System configuration.
*   **Recycle Bin**: Recover deleted items.
*   **Activity Log**: Security audit trail.
